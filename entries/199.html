Events & Observations

This entry contains remarks related to the following subject: Sea Ice 
AM
Light breezes from W. by N. with generally gloomy and cloudy weather. Very light snow at 5. Rising 
barometer and falling temperature. Early daylight at 7.15. Sounded at noon in 30 fathoms. Green mud. 
No indicated drift.

PM
Weather clearing, so as to show stars after 4. Variable light winds until 7, from which time forward the 
wind remained steady at W.N.W.

Moon 23° S. 
Last quarter
